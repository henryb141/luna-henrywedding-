Getting Here
Travel & Accommodation
By Car
St Audries Park is located off the A39 between Williton and Watchet.

Postcode for sat nav: TA4 4DP

Parking is available on site.
By Train
The nearest station is Taunton, served by Great Western Railway from London Paddington (approx. 1hr 45min).

Taxis and car hire are available from Taunton station. We recommend booking in advance.
Staying Over
St Audries Park has over 30 en-suite bedrooms on site, reserved exclusively for our wedding. Room pricing is available on request — please speak to us directly.

Nearby options:
Swain House B&B — award-winning boutique B&B in Watchet, 3.5 miles away

The Georgian House — Georgian guesthouse in Watchet town centre

Steps Farmhouse B&B — 4-star B&B in Minehead, approx 20 mins away
